Kyoto City Bus

Kyoto buses operate from approximately 6:00 to 23:00, though some run earlier or later depending on the route. At the Kyoto Station Bus Information Center you can get bus maps, buy tickets, and inquire about additional travel information.We recommend you ask an officer at the bus station or police box if you cannot find your destination or if you got lost. If you plan to be traveling by bus a lot, make a Day Pass Tickets which help you to travel more convenient and lower cost.
