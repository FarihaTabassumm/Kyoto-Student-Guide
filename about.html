About Us

Welcome to Kyoto Student Guide!
We are Group 5 from the Leadership Theory course at the Kyoto College of Graduate Studies for Informatics (KCGI). Our mission is to provide comprehensive and up-to-date information to help students, especially international ones, navigate life in Kyoto. As students ourselves, we understand the challenges and excitement of settling into a new environment, and we are here to make that transition smoother for you. Our website offers a range of resources, including guides on local culture, transportation, housing, dining, and academic support. We also highlight student-friendly events, Muslim-friendly facilities, and services, ensuring inclusivity and a warm welcome for everyone. Kyoto Student Guide is your go-to resource for making the most of your time in this beautiful city. Explore, connect, and thrive with us!
